Overview

In a kingdom long fallen, five beasts devoured the fragments of the royal crown and buried them deep in the wilds. You are a dreamer who followed the fragments into this overgrown, dangerous land.

Pocket Wilds is a complete, self-contained GBC-style action RPG built in a single HTML file. Explore a living pixel world that grows crueler the farther you roam. Slay beasts to earn Lair Keys, then descend into tense turn-based boss battles beneath each conquered lair. Forge your weapon stronger, collect relics and gear, choose from four distinct classes, and chase the shattered crown.

Features

Real-time Exploration

Classic top-down action with smooth movement, pixel-perfect collision, and a living world across five distinct biomes.

Turn-based Boss Battles

Face five memorable lair bosses in tense, dice-influenced turn-based combat with dodging, items, and high stakes.

Four Unique Classes

Knight, Lancer, Mystic, and the unlockable Shade — each with distinct weapons, playstyles, and class abilities.

Weapon Forging

Collect weapon shards at campfires and from chests to upgrade your sword, spear, or wand up to level 6.

Relics & Gear System

Discover and equip helms, armor, legwear, and charms with quality tiers. Some are powerful relics.

Achievements & Codex

Track your progress with 40+ achievements, a living bestiary, treasure catalog, and hidden lore stones.

Daily Wilds + Leaderboards

Challenge yourself with a new seeded run every day and compete on the global Hall of Legends.

Fully Self-Contained

One HTML file. No install, no dependencies. Works offline. Save & continue supported.
